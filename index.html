Intrusive Thought

A real Gemma 2 has a separate dial for each concept it knows — one for “the Golden Gate Bridge,” one for “drugs,” one for “death.” Pick a thought and turn its dial: to the right until the model can’t think of anything else, or to the left until it can’t say it at all — and squirms around the word it’s plainly thinking of, reaching for any euphemism but the real one.

loading…

pick a thought, then drag the dial

the model, left alone
